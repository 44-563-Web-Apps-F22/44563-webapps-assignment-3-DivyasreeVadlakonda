Divya Sree Vadlakonda

National Museum New Delhi

This museum is one of the beautiful places in India to visit.
It consists of Tanjore paintings,epigraphy,antroplogy,Mourya and Satavahana art galleries.
Many decorative items such as Miniature paintings gallery,Mughal miniature paintings and Buddhist Artecrafts gallery are also present.
